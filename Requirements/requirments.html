We appreciate you and your time that you will be putting into building the solution.

Task

Find the project folder already setup for you in the root of this folder.

You will be building a ‘Add to Cart & Place Order’ system for an e-commerce website. There will be a showcase of different products. You have to choose the products from this showcase and add them to the cart to place an order.

Here’s the specs from your project manager. You will be building the frontend for the above mentioned feature.

  1. The page layout is separated into 2 distinct sections:
    • Navbar - contains the logo of the company and the cart button.
    • Product Showcase - product cards are shown in grids.
  1. Cart - selected products show up here and the order total can be seen. The cart is hidden until a cart button is clicked. The cart should show up as a card from the right of the screen. Add a badge (count of products in the cart) to the cart-icon
  1. Product cards must contain the following:
    • image
    • title
    • price
    • add to cart button which should convert into a quantity add/subtract button upon clicking.
  1. Once a product is added to cart, the product must be displayed in the cart. The cart should contain the following:
    1. Cart Items
      • selected quantity, it should have the ability to add or subtract quantity from right there.
      • Price of item.
      • Subtotal (Price x Quantity) for the particular item.
      • Delete symbol/button to remove the particular item.
    1. Total invoiced amount for all the items summed together.
    1. ‘Checkout’ button to show a dummy modal saying ‘Your order has been placed!’.
    1. ‘Clear Cart’ button to clear the entire cart selection.
  1. Application should be responsive to DTM (Desktop, Tablet, Mobile)
  1. [OPTIONAL] Add some sort of testing, might be unit tests/snapshot tests/integration tests etc
